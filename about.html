About Asaar e Noor

History And Heritage
Asaar e Noor Lahore or simply ASN , is synonymous with Lahore. Among the most famous of Lahore schools, it is among the first educational institutions that were established in the Punjab.

ASN has long established traditions of consistent academic excellence and co-curricular activities and has made life at ASN quite colourful and vibrant. The School thus provides
ample opportunities to its students to discover themselves, to explore their potential and prepares them for the challenges of life.

Eligibility Criteria
For Secondary Education
Age: Candidate's Age should be between 10 - 15.
Marks: Candidate should have more the 60% marks in last class.

For Higher Education
Age: Candidate's Age should be between 15 - 18.
Marks: Candidate should have more the 60% marks in last class.
